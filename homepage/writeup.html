Writeup

Exercise 1: Typography Improvements

  • Variable Widths vs. Geometric Sans: Replaced the old "Trebuchet MS" system font with 'Outfit', a geometric sans-serif that aligns better with the modern "Kawaii" aesthetic and offers more consistent character widths.
  • Heading Personality: Swapped the standard "Segoe UI" headings for 'Quicksand', whose rounded terminals reinforce the soft, friendly vibe of the site, creating a distinct visual voice absent in the previous generic type stack.
  • Readability & Spacing: While the old line-height was decent (1.65), I increased it to 1.7 and added more padding to the "widget" containers to let the text breathe against the new high-contrast white backgrounds.

Exercise 2: Color Improvements

  • Reducing Visual Noise: Replaced the previous background's complex stack of 6 overlapping gradients (radial, conic, and linear) with a simplified, solid Lavender Blush + dot pattern to reduce eye strain and visual clutter.
  • 60-30-10 Rule: Strictly applied the rule: 60% Lavender/Azure background, 30% White content boxes (widgets), and 10% Amaranth Pink/Moss Green accents effectively correcting the previous design where "Butter", "Cream", "Peach", and "Coral" competed for dominance.
  • Card Contrast: The old design used a semi-transparent white surface (0.82 opacity). I switched to fully opaque white (#ffffff) content boxes with Moss Green borders, significantly improving text contrast and separation from the background.
